Benefiting from independence

For companies that want the flexibility to work with independent plan providers of their choice—together with the services of a trusted custodian—our approach is ideal. Each organization delivers on its strength, giving employers and their teams the freedom to focus on what they do best.

Leadership

For more than 40 years, Schwab has been a leader in the financial industry and an advocate for the individual investor.1 We entered the retirement plan services business in 1992 and remain committed to the industry—now and in the future. Schwab’s management team includes former recordkeeping business leaders with both proven experience in the retirement business and the ability to understand and anticipate industry trends.


Expertise

Schwab’s extensive experience, combined with our breadth of capabilities, enable us to see the complete picture of the retirement plan industry. We know the retirement plan business inside and out. In ways that others can’t, we draw on our deep institutional knowledge to achieve a depth of insight that sets us apart as an industry leader. While working with independent recordkeepers, we can deliver on our long-standing history as an advocate for the individual investor and plan participant.


Strength

Schwab is dedicated to protecting account assets and is a name that retirement plan sponsors, employers, and participants can depend upon. With Schwab, we want you to have the highest level of confidence that you are working with a prominent brand focused on supporting your needs as a plan sponsor and those of your plan participants.
