Sustainability

CBRE is committed to sustainability and driving improvements in the way buildings are built, leased, managed, occupied and sold. We offer a comprehensive range of services to assist clients, like Longbow Enterprises, develop and maintain a sustainable approach to property management and operations. Our aim is to enhance the efficiency of your buildings, reduce environmental footprints and operating costs, and ultimately add value to your business.

Our extensive track record includes providing consulting services to developers, investors and major occupiers to help them understand their sustainability related business risks and opportunities and realise competitive advantages

from implementing appropriate strategies. CBRE’s Assessor team provides NABERS ratings to more buildings and portfolios than any other team in Australia. This team also provides building improvement plans, NABERS tracking, and monthly monitoring reports, further demonstrating their technical know-how and ability to deliver on high quality client services.

We have aligned our Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) with Green Star Performance to ensure that as we manage assets, we are aligned with GBCA’s Performance based rating tool, and aligned with industry best practice. CBRE welcomes the opportunity to discuss our sustainability services with Longbow Enterprises.
