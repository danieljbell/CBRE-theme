Building Optimisation

99 ELIZABETH STREET, SYDNEY
7,751sqm NLA

A prime boutique 15 level commercial office building comprising basement and ground floor retail and 14 upper levels of high quality office accommodation. The appointed CBRE management team on 99 Elizabeth Street have successfully implemented a program of effective partner management to ensure optimal efficiency and drive down costs.

[CBRE] has added considerable value to the management of the building... [they were] instrumental in the tender of HVAC, Fire, BMS and access control and through negotiating contracts with service providers, [which] assisted us in making significant savings.

Bill Jenkings, Director - Kyko Group, 99 Elizabeth Street.

Driving Performance

In 2015, post redevelopment, this asset had a preliminary NABERS energy rating of 2.5 stars. The CBRE management team have deployed several initiatives to optimise asset performance, reduce energy consumption and drive down costs. The team have achieved the following outstanding results:

  • 6 x half star jump in NABERS energy rating to 5.0 stars with operational expenditure of $48,000 and no capital spend
  • NABERS commitment agreement of 4.0 well exceeded
  • Overall, energy consumption on the asset has reduced by 166,800 kWh or 14.1%.
  • This equates to $25,000 in electricity and $8,000 in Gas.
