Pulse

Our PULSE platform powers the entire spectrum of how we manage property, it integrates and connects every facet of the facilities, property management and accounting processes.

  • Help Desk

    Service requests can be instantly logged, acknowledged, allocated and tracked online or via our dedicated 1300 number.

  • Technical Services

    Fast and efficient technical management across risk, maintenance and compliance.

  • Property Management

    Used daily by our property managers to capture, manage and store critical documentation relating to financial transactions (cash and accrual), properties, leases and tenants.

  • Contractor Management

    Self-administrated contractor portal featuring an induction platform across health, safety, environmental and compliance topics.

  • Financial Performance

    Benchmarking to PCA standards, asset budget or portfolio targets.
