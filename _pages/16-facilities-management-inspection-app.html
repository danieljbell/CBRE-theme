Facilities Management Inspection App

Designed to manage and reduce risk, our inspection app is used by our facility managers to:

  • Conduct audits and routine inspections.
  • Upload imagery and commentary on-the-go while on-site to cross-reference at any time.
  • Predefine audit lists per property to capture unique characteristics of and service performance at each site.
  • Integrate with PULSE to assign actions to the relevant personnel.
  • Quickly and efficiently collate content; providing you with visual representation of your asset.
  • Save time allowing them to spend more time focusing on delivering an enhanced tenant experience and less time on the administration.
